Welcome to Incluvate

Incluvate is a bold space where we rethink disability, challenge stigma, and build an inclusive tomorrow. At Incluvate, we're not asking for space - we're building it through innovation, truth, and unwavering dignity. Whether it's respect, mobility, work, touch, or love - we talk about it all, without filters, fear, or fake inspiration.

Recognized by ENIL

European Network on Independent Living

A Proud Member of a Global Movement

We are honored to be recognized by the European Network on Independent Living (ENIL). This membership signifies our commitment to upholding the principles of independent living, social inclusion, and the rights of all disabled people.

ENIL is a Europe-wide network of disabled people, with members throughout Europe, promoting the right to independent living. This partnership connects us to a global community, allowing us to learn from and contribute to the worldwide movement for disability rights.

Our Causes

We work on multiple fronts to challenge disability stigma and build inclusive communities through advocacy, education, and direct action.

Promoting Inclusive Employment

Working to remove physical, social, and digital barriers that prevent full participation in society.

Providing Mobility & Assistive Support

Distributing wheelchairs, assistive devices, and other accessibility tools. Helping individuals gain independence and move freely in their communities.

Community Support

Creating safe spaces and peer support networks for disabled individuals to connect and share experiences.
